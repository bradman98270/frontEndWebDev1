there is no one-size-fits-all solution for clearing acne.

Skin care products alone are not enough; you need to know what products you need based on your skin and acne type, and the products need to be adjusted based on your skin's response to them. Dermatologists give you prescription medications or products, but they are not with you every step of the way to monitor your progress.

at Pure Skin Lounge we offer:

  • Your Personal Skin Coach. You don't have to deal with your acne by yourself. Your treatment at the Acne Clinic will be guided by an acne expert. Through the process, you will be educated on your acne type, actively monitor your progress and adjust your product and treatment regime based on your skins response.
  • Customized Acne Treatments. Your acne treatments will be customized for you every visit. The types of treatments you may receive include: deep pore cleansing acne facials, extractions, chemical peels and hydrating enzyme masks.
  • Professional Strength Skin Care. Along with your treatments, you will be recommended a skin care regime based on your specific acne and skin type.
  • A Clear Skin Diet Plan. Your diet affects your skin. We will educate you on what foods may be aggravating your acne and devise a clear skin diet to help stop your acne from the inside out.
  • On-Call Support. We care about each and every client and are always available to answer your questions. You can email us anytime at pureskinlounge@gmail.com and receive a response from us by phone or email within 24 hours.
